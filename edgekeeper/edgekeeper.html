The Institution

The market does not create
emotional traders.

EdgeKeeper is not a trading platform. Not a signal provider. Not a journal. It is a private mentorship institution built on one foundational truth: traders do not fail from lack of knowledge — they fail from lack of identity under pressure.

We are in the business of building that identity. Through deep behavioral observation, private mentorship, and a discipline engine that compounds over time, we transform how traders relate to risk, loss, pressure, and themselves.

You will not learn to predict markets here. You will learn to trust yourself inside them.

The Process

Your mentor opens with a single question. Your answer reveals everything. No surveys. No checkboxes. The profile builds itself, invisibly.

Dynamic Protection

Position sizing adapts to your psychology.

On a calm, disciplined day — normal risk. After three consecutive losses with elevated emotional state — Mike quietly reduces your maximum allowed size. Not as punishment. As protection.

Emotional Lock System

Five levels.
One objective.

The lock never touches your broker. It controls EdgeKeeper's execution layer — so the conversation never stops, only the damage does.

Level 1
Gentle notice. No restrictions. Mike mentions what he sees.
Level 2
Confirmation required. You must explain your reasoning before proceeding.
Level 3
Mandatory conversation. Mike says: "Talk to me for five minutes first."
Level 4
15-minute session lock. Trading paused. The conversation continues.
Level 5
"Today's version of you shouldn't be making financial decisions."
